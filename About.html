About the Artist

Sarah

Corvallis, OR

Hi! I've been working with glass for over five years now, ever since I took a glass arts class at my high school. Once I graduated, I started working in my own home studio, buying my own glass and making mosaics. I get most of my glass from Sacramento, California and Portland, Oregon. The glass I use is a mixture of mainly Bullseye and Oceanside. I'm currently attending college at Oregon State University studying psychology.
